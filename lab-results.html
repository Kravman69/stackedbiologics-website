How to read a batch ID

Every shipment carries a batch identifier in the format SB-[compound]-[lot number]. Match the batch ID on your material to the row above to find its testing record.

Testing approach

Lots are submitted to independent third-party laboratories for analysis appropriate to the material class — identity and purity assays for peptides, identity and potency for research solutions.

Traceability

We maintain transparency on sourcing, testing protocols, and quality documentation so laboratory customers can meet their internal procurement requirements.
