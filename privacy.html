What we collect

When you contact us or place an order we collect the information you provide: name, email, institution or lab name, and order details. Our site does not use advertising trackers.

To respond to inquiries, fulfill orders, provide batch documentation, and — if you subscribe — send catalog and testing updates. We do not sell or share your information with third parties except as required to fulfill an order (e.g., shipping carriers).

Order records are retained for traceability of batch documentation. To request a copy or deletion of your information, email info@stackedbiologics.com.
