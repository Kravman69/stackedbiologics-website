Research context

GHK-Cu is the copper complex of the tripeptide glycyl-L-histidyl-L-lysine. It is one of the most-studied copper peptides in skin, collagen, and tissue-repair research (CAS 89030-95-5).

This is the lyophilized powder format for reconstitution; a ready-to-use aqueous version is also available on our GHK-Cu (aqueous) page. See published studies on our research library.

Every batch is third-party tested; a COA is available on request per batch. Sold for laboratory research use only.

Laboratory handling notes

  • Store sealed, cool, and protected from light to limit copper-complex degradation; reconstitute with an appropriate research vehicle when needed.
  • Use clean laboratory technique when aliquoting; copper–peptide solutions may stain materials — use appropriate labware.
  • Recommended storage: ≤ −20 °C for the lyophilized solid; protect from light.
  • Follow institutional chemical hygiene and copper-containing waste disposal practices.
