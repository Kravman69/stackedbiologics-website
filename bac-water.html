Research context

Bacteriostatic water is sterile water containing 0.9% benzyl alcohol as a bacteriostatic agent. In the laboratory it is used as a reconstitution vehicle for lyophilized research peptides, allowing multiple withdrawals from a vial over time.

Supplied as an ancillary lab supply for laboratory research use only.

Laboratory handling notes

  • Store at controlled room temperature; do not freeze.
  • Use aseptic technique when withdrawing; wipe the stopper with an alcohol swab before each use.
  • Discard according to institutional laboratory waste procedures.
  • For laboratory reconstitution use only — not for human or veterinary use.
