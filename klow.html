Research context

KLOW combines four well-studied research peptides — BPC-157, TB-500, GHK-Cu, and KPV — co-lyophilized together in one vial. It's supplied as a convenience format for laboratory work that would otherwise require reconstituting four separate vials.

Laboratory handling notes

  • Store in a cool, dry place in the original sealed packaging; avoid humidity and elevated temperature.
  • Reconstitute once with bacteriostatic water; confirm stability of the combined solution under your method conditions.
  • Use standard peptide-handling PPE and clean technique to limit contamination of research lots.
