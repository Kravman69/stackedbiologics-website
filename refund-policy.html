Refund policy

When we issue refunds, and how to request one.

Last updated: July 24, 2026.

Before it ships

If you cancel an unpaid or unshipped order, or we cancel it (out of stock, failed verification), any payment you already sent is refunded in full.

Damaged, wrong, or lost

If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, or does not arrive, contact info@stackedbiologics.com with your order number and batch ID within 7 days of delivery (or expected delivery). We will replace the item or issue a refund.

Opened research materials

Because these are research materials, we cannot accept returns of opened or used product for safety and integrity reasons. Issues with a specific lot are handled case-by-case under the terms above.

How refunds are issued

Refunds are sent back through the same method you paid (for example, Venmo), typically within 2 business days of approval. We will confirm by email to the address on your order.

Questions

Reach out any time — most concerns are resolved within one business day. Please contact us before opening a payment dispute so we can help directly.
