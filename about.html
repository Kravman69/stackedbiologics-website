About Stacked Biologics

A US-based supplier of compounds and peptides, built on one principle: investigators should know exactly what is in the vial.

Why we exist

The research-material market is crowded with vendors offering uneven quality, generic paperwork, and no traceability. Stacked Biologics was founded to do the opposite: a focused catalog of literature-documented compounds, each listed by public identity data — CAS, formula, class, form — with independent third-party testing tied to batch IDs.

How we work

We keep the catalog deliberately small. Every listing leads with compound identity and published research context; unknowns are marked explicitly rather than invented. Lots are submitted to independent laboratories for analysis appropriate to the material class, and batch documentation is published on our Lab Results page as it is issued.

Who we serve

Qualified researchers, independent laboratories, and institutions that require documented, traceable materials. All products are supplied strictly for laboratory research use — see the full disclaimer.

Documented identity

Every compound is listed by public CAS, formula, and class — never marketing names alone.

Independent testing

Third-party analytical testing on lots and batches. Never self-certified.

US-based fulfillment

Domestic handling with full tracking and batch identifiers on every shipment.
