Research context

BPC-157 (Body Protection Compound 157; also referred to as Bepecin) is a synthetic pentadecapeptide corresponding to a fragment sequence associated with a larger protein studied in gastric-juice research. PubChem and related chemical registries list CAS 137525-51-0, molecular formula C62H98N16O22, and molecular weight approximately 1419.5 g/mol.

Published experimental literature has examined BPC-157 in non-clinical models related to tissue and vascular biology. Stacked Biologics supplies BPC-157 as a research chemical for laboratory investigation—analytical work, in vitro systems, and other controlled research uses. Material is provided as a laboratory solid (e.g. lyophilized peptide stock).

Laboratory handling notes
