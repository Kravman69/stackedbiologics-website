Questions

Frequently asked questions

How do I place an order?
Add compounds to your cart and check out with your name and shipping address. You’ll get an order number right away, then pay by Venmo. Full steps are on the how to order page.
How do I pay?
Payment is by Venmo to @ProjectStacked. At checkout you get a QR code and an order number — add your name and order number in the Venmo notes so we can match your payment. We ship once payment clears.
How much do compounds cost, and are there discounts?
Prices are listed on each product page, with multi-buy discounts of up to 20% off. Enter your email on any product page to unlock member pricing across every compound.
How fast do you ship, and from where?
Orders are processed within 1 business day and ship directly from our warehouse in Utah Valley with full tracking and a batch ID on every shipment. Standard delivery is 2-day. We currently ship within the United States only.
What if my order arrives damaged, wrong, or is lost?
Contact info@stackedbiologics.com with your order number and batch ID within 7 days and we’ll replace or refund it. Full terms are on our shipping & returns page.
Are the compounds third-party tested?
Yes. Every lot is third-party tested by HPLC and ships with a Certificate of Analysis. COAs are posted on our COA’s page, and you can request a specific lot’s COA before ordering.
How should materials be stored?
Each compound page includes storage notes appropriate to its form (lyophilized solid, solution, or aqueous vehicle). Follow your institution’s chemical hygiene and storage procedures.
Are these products for human use?
No. All Stacked Biologics products are sold strictly for research and laboratory use only. They are not for human consumption and are not a drug, cosmetic, or dietary supplement. See the full disclaimer.
Who can purchase from Stacked Biologics?
Qualified researchers and institutions. By purchasing, the buyer represents that materials will be handled under appropriate laboratory controls and will not be redistributed for personal, cosmetic, dietary, or clinical use.
