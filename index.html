Questions

Frequently asked questions

How do I verify the purity of a compound?
Every lot is intended to undergo independent third-party analytical testing. Batch-level documentation is listed on our COA’s page, and investigators may request lot-level documentation directly.
What documentation comes with each compound?
Each compound page lists compound identity — CAS, molecular formula, approximate molecular weight, class, and material form — plus lab storage notes and a literature-framed research summary. Batch IDs tie each shipment to its testing record.
Are these products for human use?
No. All Stacked Biologics products are sold strictly for research and laboratory use only. They are not for human consumption and are not a drug, cosmetic, or dietary supplement. See the full disclaimer.
Where are orders fulfilled from?
Orders are fulfilled domestically in the United States with controlled handling, full tracking, and batch identifiers on every shipment.
How should materials be stored?
Each compound page includes storage notes appropriate to its form (lyophilized solid, solution, or aqueous vehicle). Follow your institution's chemical hygiene and storage procedures.
How do I get pricing?
Pricing is currently provided by quote. Email info@stackedbiologics.com with the compound and quantity — or use the chat in the corner — and we respond within one business day. See how to order.
How fast do you ship?
Standard shipping is 2-day, with orders processed within 1 business day — tracked, from US facilities. For expedited needs, use the live chat in the corner.
Who can purchase from Stacked Biologics?
Qualified researchers and institutions. By purchasing, the buyer represents that materials will be handled under appropriate laboratory controls and will not be redistributed for personal, cosmetic, dietary, or clinical use.
