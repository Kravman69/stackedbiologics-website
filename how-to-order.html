How to order

Ordering is handled directly by email while our online store is in development. Three steps, one business day turnaround.

1 · Request a quote

Email info@stackedbiologics.com with the compound, quantity, and your lab or institution name.

2 · Confirm

We reply within one business day with availability, pricing, batch information, and payment instructions.

3 · Receive

Orders ship domestically with full tracking. Every shipment carries a batch ID tied to its testing record.

Eligibility

All products are sold strictly for research and laboratory use only. By ordering, the buyer represents that they are a qualified researcher or institution and that materials will be handled under appropriate laboratory controls. See the full disclaimer and terms of service.
