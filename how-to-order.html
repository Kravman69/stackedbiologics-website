How to order

Build your order in the cart, submit it, and we’ll reach out to confirm availability and arrange payment — three steps, one business day turnaround.

1 · Submit your order

Browse the catalog, add your compounds and quantities to the cart, then submit at checkout. Prefer to write us? Email info@stackedbiologics.com or use the live chat with the compound and quantity.

2 · We reach out for payment

We reply within one business day with availability, your total, batch information, and payment instructions. No payment is taken up front — we arrange it directly with you.

3 · Receive

Orders process within 1 business day and ship with 2-day standard delivery, fully tracked. Every shipment carries a batch ID tied to its testing record. Need it faster? Use the live chat for expedited help.
