How to order

Order through the cart — add your compounds, check out, and pay by Venmo with the order number we give you. Questions? Email us below. We reply within one business day.

1 · Add to cart & check out

Pick your compounds, add them to the cart, and check out with your name and shipping address. You get an order number right away.

2 · Pay by Venmo

At checkout you get our Venmo QR code (@ProjectStacked). Pay, then paste your name and order number into the Venmo notes so we can match it — one tap with the Copy button.

3 · Delivered in 2 days

Once payment clears we ship within 1 business day, 2-day tracked delivery. Need it faster? Live chat.

We accept Venmo, Zelle, and Cash App.

Venmo Zelle Cash App

Venmo is the default at checkout. Prefer Zelle or Cash App? Message us in the live chat or reply to your receipt email and we’ll send the details.
