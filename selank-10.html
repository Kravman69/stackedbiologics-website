Research context

Selank is a synthetic heptapeptide derived from the endogenous immunomodulatory peptide tuftsin. It is studied in anxiety, cognition, and neuropeptide-signaling research (CAS 129954-34-3).

See published studies and links on our research library.

Every batch is third-party tested; a COA is available on request per batch. Sold for laboratory research use only.

Laboratory handling notes

  • Store as a lyophilized peptide solid: minimize moisture exposure and use dry labware when weighing or reconstituting for research assays.
  • Recommended storage: ≤ −20 °C in a sealed container, protected from light, unless lot documentation specifies otherwise.
  • Choose reconstitution solvents and concentrations appropriate to the specific in vitro or analytical method.
  • Follow institutional chemical hygiene plans, PPE requirements, and waste disposal rules for peptide materials.
