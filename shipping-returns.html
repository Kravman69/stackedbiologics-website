Policy

Shipping & returns

Standard 2-day US shipping, 1-business-day turnaround, full tracking and batch identifiers on every order.

Shipping

Orders are packed and shipped directly from our warehouse in Utah Valley with full tracking. Materials are packaged appropriately for their class and form (lyophilized solid, solution, or aqueous vehicle). Each shipment includes its batch ID so the material can be matched to its testing record on our Lab Results page.

Orders are processed within 1 business day; standard delivery is 2-day, fully tracked. For expedited requirements, reach us on live chat or at info@stackedbiologics.com.

Returns

Because these materials are sensitive to handling and storage conditions, opened products cannot be returned. If a shipment arrives damaged, incorrect, or inconsistent with its documentation, contact info@stackedbiologics.com within 7 days of delivery with your order number and batch ID — we will replace the material or refund the order.

Refunds

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5 business days of approval. If a replacement is preferred, it ships at no charge with the next processing cycle. Refund requests must reference the order number.

Lost packages

Every order ships with tracking. If tracking shows no movement for 5 business days, or a package is marked delivered but not received, email us with your order number — we will open a carrier trace and either reship or refund once the trace concludes. You will not be left waiting on the carrier alone.

Cancellations

Orders can be cancelled at no charge any time before shipment — email or use live chat with your order number. Once a tracking number has been issued, the order falls under the returns terms above.

Order processing

Orders are reviewed and processed within 1 business day. During high-volume periods or when a lot is being restocked, processing may take slightly longer — we’ll notify you by email if so.

Lost or delayed packages

Once a package is handed to the carrier, delivery timing is in their hands. If tracking shows no movement for 5+ business days, email info@stackedbiologics.com with your order number and we’ll open a carrier trace. Packages confirmed lost in transit are replaced or refunded.

Refused or undeliverable shipments

Shipments returned to us as refused or undeliverable (incorrect address, unclaimed) can be reshipped once a corrected address and any reshipment postage are provided. Notify us within 14 days.

Cancellations

Orders can be cancelled for a full refund any time before they ship. Once a tracking number is generated, the order is in fulfillment and the returns terms above apply.

Refunds

Approved refunds (lost-in-transit, damaged, incorrect, or pre-shipment cancellations) are issued to the original payment method within 5 business days of approval. Because payment is arranged directly, refund timing to your account depends on your payment provider.

International shipping & customs

We currently fulfill domestic US orders only. We do not ship internationally at this time.

Damaged or incorrect orders

Photograph the packaging and labels as received and include the images with your email. Claims are acknowledged within one business day.
