Cookie policy

How we use cookies and browser storage — and how you can control them.

Last updated: July 24, 2026.

What we store

Stacked Biologics uses a small number of cookies and browser local-storage entries to make the site work. We do not sell this data.

Essential storage

These are required for core features and cannot be turned off without breaking the site:

Cart — remembers the items in your cart between pages and visits. Age & RUO acknowledgment — remembers that you confirmed you are 21+ and understand the research-use-only terms, so you are not prompted every page. Member discount — remembers that you unlocked multi-buy pricing after entering your email.

Analytics (optional)

We may use privacy-respecting analytics (Google Analytics 4) to understand aggregate, anonymized traffic — pages viewed and general location. This is never used to identify you individually. If analytics is enabled, its cookies are set only for that purpose.

Live chat

Our live-chat provider (Tawk.to) sets its own cookies to maintain your chat session. See their privacy policy for details.

Managing cookies

You can clear or block cookies and site data at any time in your browser settings. Clearing storage will reset your cart, discount unlock, and the age acknowledgment. For more on the data we handle, see our privacy policy.
